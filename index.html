Background

Finance, Business, Law

Jay Haws is a JD/MBA candidate at Wake Forest Law building toward a corporate transactional and business litigation practice. A Finance degree from the University of Minnesota and a concurrent MBA give his legal training a business-first foundation.

At Wake Forest, Jay has earned CALI recognition in Contracts and Corporate Governance, serves as Problem Chair of the Transactional Law Team, and edits the Journal of Business & Intellectual Property Law. His coursework spans M&A, Corporate Tax, and Commercial Transactions Drafting.

He founded and ran the Stillwater Loggers, a collegiate summer baseball program, for four years—while also gaining litigation experience at Lester Law and working in guest services with the Minnesota Twins. Sports is a genuine part of his background, not just a specialty label.
