Background

From the Field to the Courtroom

Jay Haws brings a unique perspective to sports law: one rooted in firsthand experience as a Division III athlete in both baseball and football. That background shapes how he approaches the legal and business challenges facing athletes and organizations today.

Currently pursuing a dual JD/MBA at Wake Forest University School of Law, Jay applies classroom learning to real-world problems as an Athletics Compliance extern at Wake Forest Athletics—advising student-athletes on NIL, eligibility, and NCAA regulations.

Before law school, Jay coached collegiate baseball and worked in guest services with the Minnesota Twins, deepening his connection to the sports industry at every level.
